# Zen Woodblock Source Inventory

Collected on: 2026-04-09

Scope used here:
- Only Chinese Chan/Zen texts composed roughly 500-1500 CE.
- Japanese and Korean holdings are in scope when they preserve Chinese texts.
- Included only when the scan or host terms appear compatible with commercial downstream work such as selling transcriptions or translations.
- Excluded when the host terms were non-commercial, permission-only, or too unclear.
- This is a sourcing memo, not legal advice.

## Not safe or not usable for this project

- CBETA text and scan derivatives when governed by CBETA's non-commercial terms.
- Mixed-provenance text mirrors that expose CBETA in the source chain, even if the host site's wrapper text advertises `CC BY` or `CC BY-SA`.
- Taiwan National Central Library viewer pages with view-only / no reposting language.
- Waseda direct archive reuse terms for resale work; use the Commons mirror instead when available.
- National Central Library rare-book catalog mirrors labeled `BY-NC-ND` or similar non-commercial / no-derivatives restrictions.
